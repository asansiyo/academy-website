Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What class levels do you teach?

We teach students from Class 6 through to post-HSC university preparation and IELTS coaching — covering all major academic levels in Bangladesh.

How many students are in each batch?

Class sizes are kept to 10–15 students to ensure every student gets adequate attention. We also offer one-on-one tutoring sessions on request.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes. All enrolled students receive our exclusive printed notes, question banks, and past paper collections — included in the monthly fee.

Are there sibling discounts?

Yes — 10% discount for the second sibling and 15% for the third. We also run scholarship programs for high-achieving students with financial need.
