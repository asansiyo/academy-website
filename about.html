Our Mission

Inspiring Every Student to Excel

Founded in 2015, Elharoon Academy was built on the belief that every student — regardless of their current academic standing — has the capacity to achieve outstanding results with the right guidance.

We pair structured academic programs with genuine mentorship, creating an environment where students don't just pass exams — they develop a lasting love of learning and the skills to succeed beyond the classroom.

Our Journey

10 Years of Impact

2015
Founded in Dhaka
Elharoon Academy opens with 4 tutors and 60 students, focusing on SSC mathematics and science.
2017
First 100% Pass Rate
All SSC students in our inaugural batch achieved minimum A grade — a milestone we've maintained since.
2019
HSC Stream Added
Expanded to cover full HSC curriculum including Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Advanced Mathematics.
2021
IELTS & University Prep
Launched dedicated university entrance coaching and IELTS preparation, placing students in top universities.
2023
New Campus Opened
Moved to a larger, purpose-built facility with air-conditioned classrooms, a library, and a resource lab.
2025
900+ Students Strong
Now serving over 900 active students across 12 subjects with a team of 35 specialist tutors.
